## Product framing

The product is:

- a readable exorcism simulator
- a solo-capable but **co-op-first** party game
- absurdist through systems output, not authored jokes
- built for friend-group blame, panic, and replay

The core business-aware design constraints are:

- replay must come from **variance and social behavior**, not from many maps
- the first 10 to 20 minutes must be legible enough for players and spectators
- one strong apartment is better than multiple thin locations
- the Early Access build should already contain the actual social product hook
- the art bar must reach a **commercial readable stylized co-op-sim level**, not a muddy prototype-horror level

## Early Access target frame

The Early Access final-build target is:

- one polished flagship location: Apartment 302
- three demons sharing that apartment:
  - Hollow Saint
  - Weeping Matron
  - The Appetite
- one replay model built from:
  - demon identity variance
  - evidence subset variance
  - manifestation pacing variance
  - different teammate compositions
- one **4-hand ritual** with Reader, Anchor, Censer, and Guard
- one signature wrong-choice failure: the **possession cascade**
- solo support plus stable **2-4 player co-op**
- a minimal but real session shell:
  - lobby
  - tool grab
  - briefing
  - play
  - debrief
  - fast replay
- a visual finish target closer to a readable stylized commercial co-op sim than to grim low-light indie horror:
  - bold silhouettes
  - clean materials
  - readable props and evidence
  - stream-friendly lighting
  - fast screenshot readability

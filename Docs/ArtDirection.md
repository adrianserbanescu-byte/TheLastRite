# Art Direction

## Goal

Match the **readability, exaggerated shapes, and playful first-person co-op tone** of [Totally Secure Airport](https://store.steampowered.com/app/4348760/Totally_Secure_Airport/) without copying its airport-specific art or comedy palette.

## Rules

- Stylized 3D, not realism-first horror
- Clear silhouettes before fine detail
- Neutral institutional interiors with bold occult accent colors
- Chunky props and readable interaction targets
- Clean materials with grime as accent, not blanket noise
- Horror should feel chaotic and legible, not murky

## Browser prototype target

- Primitive-based rooms and props should already read as:
  - readable
  - low-clutter
  - stream-friendly
  - first-person playable
- Every interaction target should be identifiable at a glance by shape, color, or glow
- Contamination escalation should shift the room mood without hiding gameplay information

## Unreal carry-forward

When the browser loop is proven, keep these art constraints in UE:

- strong silhouettes
- limited material noise
- bold signature colors per demon
- clean UI-over-world separation
- readable ritual FX
